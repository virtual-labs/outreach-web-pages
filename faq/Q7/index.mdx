---
title: Q07. What are the system requirements for an Online Virtual Lab Workshop?
---

For an  Online Virtual Labs workshop to be conducted, your
college/institute would have to meet the following system
requirements: 
  
  - The institute should be a Central/State University or 
institute/college approved by AICTE/UGC.

  - Each participant should have access to a reliable Internet connection or link.

  - Browser: Firefox and Chrome.
