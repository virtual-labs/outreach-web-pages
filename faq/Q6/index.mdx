---
title: Q06. What are the other requirements for a Onsite Virtual Labs Workshop?
---

For a Virtual Labs workshop to be conducted, your
college/institute would have to meet the following
requirements:

 - The [Virtual Labs](http://vlab.co.in/) web page should 
be accessible from the browser.

 - The college/institute should be a Central/State University 
or institute/college approved by AICTE/UGC.

 - For onsite workshops, the college/institute must have 
an auditorium with a seating capacity of at least 100 and a
designated/common lab space having 35 PCs or more.
