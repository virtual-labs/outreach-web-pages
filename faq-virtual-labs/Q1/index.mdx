---
title: Q01. What are Virtual Labs?
---
Virtual Labs are intended to augment the learning of science and engineering subjects through performing experiments. The experiments are designed either as simulations or as remote triggered. A remote triggered lab allows a user to connect to real equipment using a web browser. There are currently around 150 and labs and 1500 experiments at various stages of development and deployment. They are currently hosted at [Vlabs](vlabs.ac.in). Virtual Labs is an initiative of Ministry of Human Resources, India.
